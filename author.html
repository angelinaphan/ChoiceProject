About The Director

Miyazaki Hayao

Japanese Anime Director

  • Born on January 5, 1941 in Tokyo, Japan
  • Took a position as an entry-level animator at Tōei Animation
  • While at Tōei, he met fellow animators Takahata Isao and Ōta Akemi. The former became a lifelong friend, collaborator, and business partner, and after a one-year courtship, became his wife.

Noteable Films

Princess Mononoke (1997)

Spirited Away (2001)

Howl’s Moving Castle (2004)

The Boy and the Heron (2023)
